The Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is a wrought iron tower that stands 1 ,063 ft (324 m) tall in Paris.

Currently, the Tower is the most-visited monument in the world with over 7 million visitors a year.

Visitors can choose to go up using the Tower lift or the stairs.

There are 1 ,665 stairs to the top.
